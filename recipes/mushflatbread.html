Caramelized Mushroom Flatbreads

Description

Caramelized Mushroom Flatbreads with Tomato, Garlicky White Sauce and Parmesan will statisfy every pizza craving in just 30 minutes! This dish is easily customizable and surprizingly easy to make! It will quickly become a staple dinner.

Ingridents

  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • Container of Button Mushrooms (8oz)
  • 1 Shallot
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Dried Oregano
  • 2 Flatbreads
  • 2 tbsp Cream Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Mozzarella
  • 1/3 cup Shredded Parmesan
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Cooking Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • Red Chili Flakes (to taste)

How to Make:

  1. Gather ingredients. Wash and dry produce
  2. Preheat oven to 450 deg F
  3. Slice tomato into rounds. Thinly slice Mushrooms. Halve, peel, and slice Shallot. Peel and finley chop Garlic
  4. In small bowl, combine tomato, a drizzle of oil, pinch of oregano, salt and pepper. Set aside to marinate
  5. Place flatbreads on baking sheet; toast on top rack for 5-7 minutes, until lightly toasted
  6. Heat a drizzle of oil in large pan over med-high heat. Add mushrooms and shallot; season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently for 5-7 minutes. Add 1/2 tsp of sugar and a splash of water. Continue cooking, stirring frequently until mixture is caramelized and jammy (1-2 minutes more). Turn off heat, remove from pan. Wipe out pan.
  7. Melt 1 tbsp in same pan over med-high heat. Add garlic, oregano, and 1 tbsp flour. Whisk constantly for about 30 seceonds. Reduce heat to med-low; whisk in cream cheese and 1/2 cup of water. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer until thickened. Remove pan from heat
  8. Heat brolier to high. Evenly top flatbreads with white sauce, marinated tomato, and caramelized mushroom mix. Sprinkle both flatbreads with Mozzarella and Parmesan. Broil until cheese melts, 1-2 minutes. Keep a close eye!
  9. Slice flatbreads and split between plates. Sprinkle with Chili Flakes if desired.
