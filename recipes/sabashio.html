Saba Shioyaki

Description

Saba Shioyaki is a popular Japanese grilled fish recipe. The name Saba Shioyaki translates to "Salt-grilled Mackerel". The dish is simiple and can be made with few ingridents. This recipe was created by Namiko Chen of Just One Cookbook.

Ingridents

  • 2 fillets mackerel
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt

How to Make:

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. In a rimmed plate, coat fish with 2 tbsp sake
  3. Pat fish dry with paper towel, discard remaining sake
  4. Sprinkle both sides of the fish with 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Let the fish sit at room temperature for 20 min. Preheat the oven to 425 deg F
  6. Pat fish with paper towels to remove excess moisture
  7. Place fish skin side down and bake for 15-20 minutes on the middle rack
  8. Serve Mackerel with steamed rice, miso soup, and vegetable sides
